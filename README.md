
# GenAI is the Enemy of Education

I am a statistician and a teacher. I see generative AI (GenAI) encroaching on both of those practices, much to the excitement of people who build the infrastructure to support GenAI and management who have a history of disregarding the needs of teachers and students alike.

The goal of this repo is to provide a summary of the current evidence for the negative impact of GenAI in the form of large language models (LLMs) on learning, teaching, training, and expertise.

This project is a conversation. If you have an argument or a source that is not included but should be, please head over to https://github.com/Tess-LaCoil/enemy-of-ed/issues/1 and add it.
